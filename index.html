Overcast

Free, secure peer-to-peer internet chat

Why Overcast?

Secure

Since Overcast uses peer-to-peer, no chat data ever goes to the server. And with the app, you can even start your own server.

Free

Overcast is, and always will be free. And it's open surce too, check it out on Github!

No Accounts

You only need the app to create servers. Other people can go to your server's link, and chat from a web browser*.

Customisable

6 different themes to choose from. Custom themes coming soon!.

*Web chat is only supported in Chrome. In Firefox, messages can be recieved but not sent.
